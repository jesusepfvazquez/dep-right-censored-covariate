# ODRCC: Robust Estimation Under **O**utcome-**D**ependent **R**ight **C**ensoring **C**ovariate

The ODRCC package implements robust and efficient estimation estimators
for regression models involving outcome-dependent right-censored
covariates. Available estimators inverse probability weighting (IPW),
augmented IPW (AIPW), and maximum likelihood (MLE).

## References

Vazquez, J. et al. (2025). \* Robust Estimation under Outcome Dependent
Right Censoring in Huntington Disease: Estimators for Low and High
Censoring Rates\*. Manuscript.
